What is SEO? hi

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving the visibility of your website in search engine results pages (SERPs).

Why is SEO Important?

SEO helps drive organic traffic to your website, improves user experience, and boosts your site's credibility.

SEO Best Practices

  • Conduct keyword research to target relevant search terms.
  • Optimize meta titles, descriptions, and headers.
  • Use alt attributes for images.
  • Ensure your site is mobile-friendly and loads quickly.
  • Create high-quality, original content.
